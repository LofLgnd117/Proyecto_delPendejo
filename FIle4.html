Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

(Insert your recipe image here)

Ingredients:

  • Brown Sugar & White Sugar
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips

Instructions:

  1. Mix wet ingredients and dry ingredients separately.
  2. Combine them together and fold in the chocolate chips.
  3. Scoop onto a baking sheet.
  4. Bake until the edges are golden but the centers are soft.
