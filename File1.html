Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter
  • Yeast

Instructions:

  1. Mix the dough and let it rise.
  2. Roll it out and add the butter, sugar, and cinnamon.
  3. Roll up the dough and cut into slices.
  4. Bake until golden brown.
