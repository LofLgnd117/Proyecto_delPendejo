Vanilla Pound Cake

(Insert your recipe image here)

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour

Instructions:

  1. Cream the butter and sugar together.
  2. Add the eggs one at a time.
  3. Stir in the vanilla and flour.
  4. Bake in a loaf pan until a toothpick comes out clean.
